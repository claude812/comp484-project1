Team Stats


1. England

They are currently the six nations team with the most tournament wins going back to when it started with only four teams. They have featured in the tournament 129 times. They have won the competition a record 29 times. They won the competition 4 times when it was called the home nations with just the original 4 teams. Then they had a big era of dominance during the 5 nations era. They managed to win the competition a record 17 times the most out of the 5 teams. Since 2000 when it became the six nations they have won it a further 7 times having won it joint most with France. They also won a world cup during the six nations era with a famous Johnny Wilkinson drop goal to cement themselves in rugby royalty. They've gone through a bit of a transition period the last 5 years but this season they look back to their best and despite a shock loss to Scotland they seem to have a strong squad that could pose a serious challenge to France this season. They will be looking to capitalise on Ireland leaving a golden generation and will be hopefull to add a tenth title and bring their total titles up to 30.



2. Wales

Another home nation Wales is also a very successful team and have had some of the most iconic players of all time they would have probably been the best team in the competition during my teen years under head coach Warren Gatland. He is probably the most iconic coach in my life time in my opinion. They have featured in the competition a total of 131 times wining it 28 times in total making them the second most successful team in the competition. They won the home nations seven times. In the five nations they got an impressive 15 titles second only to England in titles won. Then they have also won the 6 nations six times. They haven't had the same success as England in the world cup but they have managed a third place position.



3. France

The first nation i've talked about that isnt a home nation and have only featured 96 times. This is a team that despite its later arrival never looked out of place and is top3 for total titles with 19 total titles. In the 5 nations they managed a total of 12 titles a huge achievement for a team that just joined when compared to how Italy have performed since joining it wil really highlight the rugby brilliance they have. They then followed it with 7 six nations titles and in my opinion will make it eight this year and win every games they currently have the best player in the world and maybe of all time in Antoine Dupont and have a very strong team. They took a different approach to youth development focusing on club rugby instead of school boy. This tactic seems to have worked brilliantly for them with the best internal league and the most profitable and highest earning. Most national teams have a rule that you must play for a team in the country to play for the national team. However a lot of the best players in the world still join this league because of the money in it even though they lose the chance to play for their country



4. Ireland

Now we get on to my home country and favourite team Ireland. They have featured 131 times in total and have been very successful in recent times but have not been brilliant historically hitting a few very low points but despite having come last the most they are the country that its been the longest since they came last. They have won the competition a total of 16 times. They only won the home nations 4 times the least amount out of the 4 teams. They have also won the 5 nations the second lowest amount of times with 6 titles. Then in the six nations they have won 6 putting them at joint second with Wales. Their recent success might be in part due to switching their club format. They went from having a big league with a load of teams to 4 teams that compete in a European club league. This led to the clubs having more funding for player development and academies leading to a clearer pathway to high level pro rugby. Despite having the smallest population in the six nations with Ireland only having a population of 6 million people compared to for example the likes of France with 68 million Ireland are currently managing to constantly punch above their weight in rugby.



5. Scotland

Scotland who are a team that have struggled for form for the last 25 years. Although they are the home to some rugby greats they just never seemed to have a complete and balanced team since i was born with just 1 or 2 star players in a team with a bunch of average ones. They are the final home nation and have played a total of 131 competitions with a total of 14 titles. They have won the home nations 9 times the most of any team. They have won the 5 nations the least amount of times with 5 titles. They have never won a six nations and dont look like they will for another while. Although for the last two decades they have looked very weak compared to how good they used to be they have a team that although i dont see winning a title it is finally strong enough that on their they that scottish team could beat anyone.



6. Italy

Unfortunately they are the least successful team in the competition. They have been in it for 26 years and are yet to get a title. They have gone from the team you would play your B team against and win 50-0 to a team that on their day can beat anyones A team. A big part of this is the new young crop of players. They have some player who are top 3 in the world un their position for example Menoncello who i believe is the best in the world in his position.
